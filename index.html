Selin Erguncu, PhD

Data Scientist

I obtained my PhD in Marketing (2015) from Koc University, Istanbul and BSc in Industrial Engineering (2009) from Istanbul Technical University. I spent two quarters as a visiting scholar at UCLA during my PhD. My research focuses on consumer decision-making, behavioral economics, marketing strategy, and social media. I have won a Marketing Science Institute research grant and a Wharton Customer Analytics Initiative data competition for my research.

After completing my PhD, I had a postdoc position in USC Marshall School of Business, and worked as a research associate in USC Marshall Center for Global Innovation (2016).

You can find me on Twitter, LinkedIn, GitHub, Instagram, and Facebook.
